Where to Eat

Tampa's Best Restaurants

Legendary Steakhouse
Bern's Steak House
One of America's most famous steakhouses — dry-aged beef, an extraordinary wine cellar, and a legendary dessert room upstairs.
📍 1208 S Howard Ave
Historic Cuban — Since 1905
Columbia Restaurant
Florida's oldest restaurant in Ybor City. Flamenco shows, legendary Cuban sandwich, and incredible sangria.
📍 2117 E 7th Ave, Ybor City
Food Hall
Armature Works
Tampa's premier food hall in a stunning historic building on the Riverwalk. Dozens of top chef concepts in one place.
📍 1910 N Ola Ave
Native Florida Cuisine
Ulele
Farm-to-table Florida cuisine in a beautifully restored historic water works building on the Hillsborough River.
📍 1810 N Highland Ave
Brunch & Coffee
Oxford Exchange
Tampa's most beautiful cafe — stunning interior, bookstore, excellent brunch, and specialty coffee.
📍 420 W Kennedy Blvd
Fine Dining
Mise en Place
Tampa's most celebrated fine dining restaurant — elegant, locally-sourced tasting menus in a sophisticated setting.
📍 442 W Kennedy Blvd

🥙 Local Tip

Tampa invented the Cuban sandwich — don't leave without trying one. La Segunda Central Bakery and the Columbia Restaurant are the gold standards. Ybor City's 7th Avenue is a must for food, culture, and people-watching.

Nearest Beaches

Gulf Beaches from Tampa

#1 US Beach — 45 min
Clearwater Beach
Consistently America's top-rated beach. Stunning white sand, Pier 60, sunsets, and a lively beachfront strip.
🚗 45 min via Courtney Campbell Causeway
Local Gem — 50 min
Indian Rocks Beach
Quieter, more residential Gulf beach with great restaurants and gorgeous sunsets. Where some of our properties are!
🚗 50 min
Classic Florida — 40 min
St. Pete Beach
Wide sandy beach with a vibrant strip of bars and restaurants. Great for a full beach day with nightlife options.
🚗 40 min
Pristine — 1 hr
Caladesi Island
Accessible only by ferry — one of the most unspoiled beaches in Florida.
🚗 + Ferry, about 1 hr total
Nature Beach — 55 min
Fort De Soto
Stunning barrier island park with crystal water, kayaking, camping, and a beloved dog beach.
🚗 55 min
State Park — 50 min
Honeymoon Island
Beautiful state park beach with nature trails, osprey nests, and a calm family-friendly atmosphere.
🚗 50 min

🌊 Beach Tip

Tampa Bay itself is not ideal for swimming but the Gulf beaches are world-class and within 45–60 minutes. Beat traffic by leaving before 9am or after 2pm. The Courtney Campbell Causeway offers stunning bay views on the way to Clearwater.

Nightlife

Tampa After Dark

Historic Entertainment District
Ybor City
Tampa's Latin Quarter transforms at night — dozens of bars, live music, clubs, and restaurants in historic brick buildings.
📍 7th Ave, Ybor City
Waterfront Bars & Restaurants
The Riverwalk
Tampa's stunning waterfront promenade lined with bars, restaurants, and venues. Beautiful at night.
📍 Downtown Tampa waterfront
Upscale Neighborhood
Hyde Park Village
Charming outdoor shopping and dining district with excellent cocktail bars for a sophisticated evening.
📍 S Dakota Ave & Swan Ave
Dive Bar Legend
The Hub
Tampa's most beloved dive bar — cheap drinks, friendly crowd, and a true local institution since 1947.
📍 719 N Franklin St
Casual Bar & Grill
Green Iguana
Multiple locations, great happy hours, and a lively crowd. A Tampa staple for casual drinks and food.
📍 Multiple Tampa locations
Live Events
Amalie Arena District
Home of the Tampa Bay Lightning. Dozens of bars cluster around the arena on game nights — electric atmosphere.
📍 401 Channelside Dr

🎉 Nightlife Tip

Ybor City is Tampa's nightlife heartbeat — especially Thursday through Saturday. It's walkable, affordable, and has something for everyone from dive bars to dance clubs. Download the Lime scooter app for easy transport between Ybor, downtown, and the Riverwalk.

📍 4810 N Himes Ave
Rock Climbing Gym

Tampa's premier indoor climbing gym with bouldering, top rope, lead walls, and fitness equipment.
📍 4421 N Himes Ave
Yoga — Drop-In Friendly

Modern yoga studio offering multiple styles with drop-in options. Great for stretching out after travel.
📍 Multiple Tampa locations
Soccer & Sports Complex
Rowdies Training Ground

🚴 Active Tip

Bayshore Boulevard is a Tampa institution — locals run, walk, and bike it daily. At 4.5 miles one way it's perfect for a longer workout with stunning views of Tampa Bay. Go early morning to beat the Florida heat.
